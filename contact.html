❓ FAQ

Is Jetmeaway free?
Yes, 100% free. We earn commission from partners when you book, but this never affects your price.
Can I book through Jetmeaway?
We're a comparison site. When you find a deal, we redirect you to the provider (e.g. Expedia) to complete the booking.
I have a booking issue.
Since bookings are made with the provider, contact them directly for cancellations, changes, or refunds.
How do you choose providers?
We partner with major, reputable providers. Results may be influenced by commercial relationships, but we aim to show the best prices.
