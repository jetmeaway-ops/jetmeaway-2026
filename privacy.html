🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we handle your information.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Jetmeaway (jetmeaway.co.uk) is a travel comparison website operated from the United Kingdom. We help users compare prices from various travel providers. Contact: waqar@jetmeaway.co.uk.

2. What We Collect

  • Email address — if you sign up for price alerts or contact us.
  • Usage data — pages visited, searches made, links clicked via cookies.
  • Device info — browser type, OS, screen resolution.

We do not collect payment information. All bookings are completed on our partners' websites.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our comparison service.
  • Send price drop alerts (if opted in).
  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Understand site usage to improve it.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies & Affiliate Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience and for affiliate tracking. When you click a travel deal, a cookie is placed by our partners (Expedia via Partnerize, Booking.com via CJ Affiliate, Travelpayouts) so we receive a commission if you book. This never affects your price. Cookies typically last 24 hours to 30 days. You can control cookies via browser settings.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site links to external travel providers. They have their own privacy policies. Partners include Expedia, Booking.com, GetYourGuide, Viator, Klook, Tiqets, Airalo, Yesim, Ekta Traveling, Economy Bookings, Localrent, Qeeq, GetRentaCar, KiwiTaxi, Welcome Pickups, GetTransfer, AirHelp, Compensair, WeGoTrip, and Aviasales.

6. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, email waqar@jetmeaway.co.uk.

7. Data Retention

Email addresses retained while subscribed. Removed within 30 days of unsubscribing. Usage data retained anonymised for up to 12 months.

8. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

9. Children

Our site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

Questions? Email waqar@jetmeaway.co.uk or visit our contact page.
